Sources

The majority of the site content is an interactive version of the Star Wars Galaxy Map, fantastic work of Willem R. van Hage. Planet details are derived from the SWAPI project - The Star Wars API, created by Paul Hallett. The planet image was taken from Wookieepedia and the background image, made by Ilnur Kalimullin, from Unsplash.
